Simulation Details

Our main showcase element, the driving simulation, was broken down into four interactive elements which are part of the Horizon system, and four objects which were meant to enhance the driving experience.

  • A. Sliding Knob
  • B. iPad showing Center Stack Display
  • C. iPad showing Cluster Screen
  • D. Reflective film simulating Heads-Up Display (HUD)
  • 1. iPhone to reflect in HUD film
  • 2. Two widescreen monitors playing a driving video
  • 3. Pedals
  • 4. Steering Wheel
